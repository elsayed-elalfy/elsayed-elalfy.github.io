AI, Machine Learning, and Trustworthy Intelligent Systems

I am a Professor of Information & Computer Science at King Fahd University of Petroleum & Minerals (KFUPM). My work advances trustworthy, privacy-aware, and deployable intelligent systems operating under real-world constraints.

Research

My research investigates methodological and applied aspects of artificial intelligence with emphasis on reliability, robustness, privacy, and secure deployment.

Core Directions

  • Trustworthy and privacy-aware learning
  • Robust computer vision systems
  • AI for security and critical infrastructure
  • Bio-inspired and optimization-based intelligence

Application Contexts

  • Intelligent transportation and driver monitoring
  • Industrial IoT and cyber-physical systems
  • Secure analytics and anomaly detection
  • Smart and sustainable environments

Selected Projects

  • Privacy-preserving learning — federated and distributed models under practical constraints.
  • Robust vision intelligence — spatiotemporal modeling for real-world variability and uncertainty.
  • Intelligent secure systems — analytics for cybersecurity and mission-critical environments.
  • Optimization-based intelligence — swarm and nature-inspired methods for complex decisions.
